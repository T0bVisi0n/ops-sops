# Lead Cleanup Rules

## Required Fields

- Company
- Website
- Country
- Contact Name
- Role
- Email
- LinkedIn
- Source
- Status

## Cleanup Rules

- Remove duplicates
- Mark generic email addresses
- Reject irrelevant businesses such as beauty salons or PMU studios
- Prioritize DACH
- Prioritize aesthetic clinics and aesthetic practices
- Mark leads without website
- Assign status: A, B, C or Reject

## Scoring Logic

- +3 aesthetic clinic
- +2 DACH
- +2 clear service offering
- +2 visible growth signals
- -3 beauty salon
- -3 no ICP fit
- -2 no valid website
